The Problem

Surgery doesn't end in the OR.
The weeks that follow are where outcomes are truly decided.

Millions
of patients leave surgery each year with no structured way to track their recovery
Hours
spent by providers fielding scattered calls and managing unorganized patient check-ins
Days
or weeks of delay before complications are caught — often at a follow-up visit too late
Patients go home anxious, uncertain, and wondering “is this normal?” Providers are overwhelmed, catching complications too late. Both sides are navigating the most critical window of recovery in the dark.
MISSION

Our Mission

To make post-surgical recovery something patients navigate with confidence, not fear — and to give providers the visibility they need without adding to their workload.

We are SutureSight. Recovery, Redefined.

Who We Serve

Built for both sides of recovery

For Patients

Confidence after surgery

Recovery can feel uncertain — the waiting, the wondering, the questions that feel too small to call about. SutureSight is designed to bring clarity to the days and weeks after a procedure, so you never feel like you're navigating it alone.

For Providers

Visibility without burden

Post-op follow-up takes time your team doesn't always have. SutureSight is built to give your practice better visibility into patient recovery — so your team spends less time chasing updates and more time on patient care.
