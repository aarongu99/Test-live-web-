Hotel Revenue Management Systems

Hotels were among the first industries to adopt airline-style yield management. Modern hotel revenue management systems (RMS) like IDeaS, Duetto, and Atomize use machine learning to dynamically price rooms based on predicted demand, competitor rates, and booking patterns.

These systems optimize for "RevPAR" (Revenue Per Available Room), balancing the tradeoff between high occupancy at lower rates and lower occupancy at premium rates. The algorithm's goal is to maximize total revenue, not just bookings.

What Determines Your Rate

Day of week, season, local events and conferences, current occupancy level, booking lead time, length of stay, room type availability, competitor rates, your booking channel (direct vs. OTA), loyalty status, and historical demand patterns for the same date range.

The OTA Factor

Online Travel Agencies (OTAs) like Booking.com, Expedia, and Hotels.com add another layer of algorithmic pricing. These platforms use their own algorithms to sort and promote properties, and the placement and visibility of a hotel listing affects the prices guests ultimately see and pay.

Hotels often have "rate parity" agreements with OTAs, meaning they can't publicly advertise lower rates on their own website. However, hotels frequently offer better deals through loyalty programs, email-only offers, or call-in rates that bypass these restrictions.

Many booking platforms now show different prices to logged-in members vs. anonymous visitors. Booking.com's "Genius" program and Expedia's membership discounts create a two-tier pricing system where the same room appears at different rates depending on your login status.

How to Get Better Hotel Rates

Book directly with the hotel and ask for a price match. Join loyalty programs for member-only rates. Check prices while logged in and logged out. Compare across multiple OTAs. Book refundable rates and rebook if prices drop. Consider calling the hotel directly ‚Äî phone agents sometimes have access to rates not available online. Avoid booking during major local events if possible.
