Ian Kovarsky

A Different Kind of Family Lawyer

When you're facing a family law matter, you need more than just legal expertise—you need someone who understands what you're going through.

Ian Kovarsky founded Kovarsky Law with a simple mission: to provide thoughtful, personalized legal representation to families in Colorado. With over 8 years of dedicated family law experience, Ian brings both professional expertise and genuine compassion to every case.

Unlike large firms where you might feel like just another case number, at Kovarsky Law you work directly with Ian throughout your entire case. This direct relationship means better communication, more consistent strategy, and ultimately better outcomes for you and your family.

Ian's practice focuses exclusively on family law matters, including divorce, child custody, child support, domestic violence protection, adoption, and prenuptial agreements. This focused approach ensures that every client benefits from deep, specialized knowledge rather than generalized legal advice.

Based in Castle Rock, Ian serves clients throughout Douglas County, El Paso County, Arapahoe County, Jefferson County, and Denver County. Virtual consultations make it easy to get started from anywhere in Colorado.

Education & Background

Ian earned his Juris Doctor from the University of Memphis Cecil C. Humphreys School of Law, where he developed a strong foundation in family law and civil litigation. Before founding Kovarsky Law, Ian honed his skills at established family law practices, handling complex cases involving high-asset divorces, contested custody matters, and domestic violence situations.

This background prepared Ian well for the realities of family law practice—where every case involves real families, real emotions, and real stakes. It's not just about knowing the law; it's about understanding how to apply it effectively while supporting clients through one of the most challenging times of their lives.
